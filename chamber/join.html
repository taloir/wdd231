Gold Membership Level

There is no fee for nonprofit buisnesses.

Non-Profit members have access to all Bronze Tier perks at no cost. Which includes the buisness being listed in the Directory, and access to posting their events publicly.

Fee = 10 Gold Pieces

Bronze Tier Members have their buisnesses listed in the Directory, which is available to anyone looking at the Chamber Site. As well as being able to post their events publicly.

Fee = 20 Gold Pieces

Silver Tier Members recieve free home page advertising. As well as Events are listed as priority once posted.

Fee = 30 Gold Pieces

In addition to free home page advertising and Event priority, Gold Tier Members can enjoy the discounted use of the Community Hall for special training or Conventions.
